# Retail Business Exploratory Data Analysis

## Overview
An exploratory data analysis of retail business data to uncover profit patterns, shipping performance, category profitability and geographical sales insights. The findings provide actionable business recommendations to improve revenue and profitability.

## Dataset
- **Domain:** Retail / E-commerce
- **Coverage:** Sales transactions, shipping modes, product categories, customer segments, city-level performance

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas & NumPy
- Matplotlib & Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Findings
- **Shipping:** Standard Class shipping generates more than 50% of total profit — the most impactful delivery mode
- **Customer Segment:** Home Office segment contributes the lowest profit — not advisable as a primary focus area
- **Product Category:** Technology is the highest performing and most profitable category — should be the main business focus
- **Geography:** Several cities show consistently low profit generation — targeted sales strategies needed to improve performance in these regions

## Business Recommendations
- Prioritise and maintain Standard Class shipping as the primary fulfilment method
- Redirect marketing investment away from Home Office segment toward Consumer and Corporate segments
- Increase stock, promotion and visibility of Technology category products
- Launch targeted campaigns in low-performing cities to drive sales growth and profitability
